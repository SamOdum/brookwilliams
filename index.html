frequently asked questions

Can we access international funding for local real estate development?

Yes. Real estate development is a solid investement and continues to attract savy investors. Ordinarily, most developers would prefer to complete their project with in-house financing or locally sourced finance. However, because of its capital intensive nature several developers have to resort to external financing to meet project deadlines. There are a number of pathways through which you can assess financing and we would love to discuss it in detail if you so desire.

Is my regular attorney suitable to handle my imternatiuonal merger and acquisition?

A number of factors are necessary consideration in choosing and appointing attorneys to act for and in your behalf. These include subject-area expertise, experiance, and scope of practice. So your regular attorneys may or may not be suitable for international mergers and acquisitions.

Do I have any legal recourse in the event of suffering financial fraud?

Yes. Most countries have in place laws and statutes specifically formulated to remedy financial fraud. So even if you have reason to believe your defrauder lives in a country different from yours it is always advisable to approach law enforcement and an established financial fraud law experts.
